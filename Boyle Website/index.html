About Us

Our Story

Our story is a simple one and begins with involvement from mutual friends. It started with a quick introduction at a local bar and then led to a conversation that lasted all night. From there the relationship grew and grew until eventually Tyler popped the question atop of Brokeoff Mountain in Lassen National Park. The proposal spoke to both Tyler and Shelby's love for the great outdoors. Some of their hobbies now include exercising together, hiking, watching baseball, cuddling with their Standard Poodle, Napoleon and occasionally tasting a fantastic beer or wine.

Shelby

Born in Chico, California Shelby Davis is the daughter to Michael and Dolores Davis. Growing up as a Chico Native, Shelby enjoyed being outdoors and playing soccer. Throughout her early 20's Shelby studied at California State University, Chico. Eventually earning her degree in Business Information Systems. It would not be until after college and a few years out in the "real world" where she would meet Tyler.

Tyler

Born in Santa Rosa, California Tyler Boyle is the son of Ellen Palazzo and Christopher Boyle. Eventually moving from Santa Rosa as a young boy to South Lake Tahoe gave Tyler is sense of adventure and love for the outdoors. Throughout his childhood Tyler enjoyed camping and the occasional video game. Eventually he would reach his 20's and also enroll at California State University, Chico where he would receive a degree in Geography and Planning.
